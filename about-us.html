Syeda Fizza Jaffery

CEO

Syeda Fizza Jaffery is a student of BS Software Engineering from Usman Institute of Technology (UIT-NED). She is currently serving as Research Intern in Micro Electronics Research Lab (MERL-UIT), the first Research Lab that aims to design First Digital Processor of Pakistan that is based on open-source RISC-V Technology. She is also a team memeber of MERL Socail Media Cell. She is fuelled by her passion for understanding the nuances of multi-language software development. She considers herself a ‘forever student,’ eager to build on both perusing professional degree along with learning new skills. Her eagerness for knowledge and determination to turn information into action has contributed to most recent success of Group in MERL that presented their research work about “Reverse Engineering of Rocket Chip” at RISC-V Summit (8th Dec,2020).
